Welcome to the Portfolio of António Rocha.

Since 2021, I have been dedicated to my role as a Quality Engineer Analyst, where I focus on ensuring the highest standards of quality and performance in every project I undertake.

Prior to my current position, I spent seven years, from 2014 to 2021, as a Front-End Developer. During this time, I honed my skills in web development, creating user-friendly and visually appealing websites.

My journey in the world of IT and technology began in 2010 when I started working as a consultant specializing in Outsystems. Over the four years in this role, I gained valuable experience in developing and implementing solutions for various clients, contributing to their digital transformation.

I am a proud alum of the Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto (ISEP), where I acquired the knowledge and foundation that have allowed me to excel in my career.

Explore my website to learn more about my professional journey and my passion for technology and quality assurance. Thank you for visiting, and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to connect.
